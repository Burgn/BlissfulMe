FAQ's

Why happiness?

We believe everyone has the right to be happy! and our dedicated staff are willing to work tirelessly to make that happen. Our website information, happiness quizzes and online forums to share stories are all here to make a healthier, happier you!.

What is happiness?

Although there is not one definition happiness is feeling of positivity that differs from person to person. Happiness makes us nicer, more hardworking and more helpful. Your happiness can help other people be happy too. Even though happiness is subjective and can arise from different thing in different people like having a good work and social life or having no job and traveling it brings us closer to one another.

Does money really make you happy?

No, research indicates that happiness brought about by money is short lived. Sure having the latest technology is great but that feeling doesn't last forever. Only people that live in poverty have a significant change in happiness when money is brought into the picture.

So what really keeps us happy?

Doing what you love! Studies indicate that participating in hobbies and events increase individual satisfaction. Building social relationships and sharing stories also increases happiness, That's why our team has created an interactive forum where you can read about other people's experiences and share your own. Games and accomplishments are sure to boost your mood, have a go with our virtual happy tree!

What makes Blissful website different?

Our website puts a focus on the concept of sharing happiness. Our philosophy is that we are happier together. Once you sign up our web page is fully customisable and gives the option of sharing happiness with your friends through social media platforms. Interactions between users support our philosophy. Various links to help-lines allow professionals to share in improving your happiness.
