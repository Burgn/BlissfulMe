Glossary

Bliss

-supreme happiness; utter joy or contentment.

Delight

-a feeling of great happiness and pleasure.

Elation

-a feeling of great happiness or excitement

Emotions

-a strong feeling deriving from one's circumstances, mood, or relationships with others.

Euphoria

-a state of intense happiness and self- confidence.

Good Life

-a life that is choice worthy or desirable, all things considered, typically including moral goodness as well as well-being.

Gross National Happiness

-a developing philosophy as well as an "index" which is used to measure the collective happiness in any specific nation.

Happiness

-a broad state of mind, such as life satisfaction, pleasure, or emotional well-being, or some combination of such states.

Happy (feeling)

-an emotion associated with smiling, and feelings like cheerfulness, joy or contentment (feeling happy is considered to be, at most, one aspect of being happy).

Joy

-the emotion of great delight or happiness caused by something exceptionally good or satisfying; keen pleasure; elation.

Life Satisfaction

-an attitude of being satisfied with one's life

Optimistic

-hopeful and confident about the future.

Pleasure

-a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment.

Well-being

-a pleasant state of psychological and physical health.
