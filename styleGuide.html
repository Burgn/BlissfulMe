Style Guide

Typography

The font to be used for almost the entire website is  “Indie Flower” in cursive, of varying sizes for the headings and body text. This is coloured either white or black. The logo is hand drawn, and is light blue (#65BCFF)

Home Page

The homepage features a centred image of a head with a tree above it. To the right of this, we have placed a gif of a growing tree, with text reading “Click the tree to activate it”. Clicking on this gif redirects the user to our page prompting the user to sign up or log in to water their happiness tree.

Navigation and Layout

There is always a navigation bar across the very top of the page featuring the Blissful Me logo. The bar is a dark green (rgb- 41, 89, 32) Clicking on the home bar will always lead back to the homepage page, as per convention. The home bar features tabs: home, facts, your tree, your say and login, all in a white “indie flower” font. The navigation bar also includes a search function on the right hand side with a text input function. The contents of the page always appear centred over the background. Each page has a unique background, based on nature, such as images of trees, all featuring a blue/green colour scheme) When scrolled over, the navigation tabs have drop down bars that are displayed vertically across the top of the page, under the navigation bar. Active pages are highlighted in blue (#65BCFF), Colour Palette (Base) The colour palette features hues of blue and green. These colours are proven to promote happiness and wellbeing.
